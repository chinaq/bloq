## A-C

|   | vanilla | deep |
|---| --- | --- |
| value update | approximate Qt = Qt + alpha(Gt-Qt) | min Loss = (Gt-Qt)^2 by gradiant descent|
| policy update | approximate p = p + beta(Gt-Qt) | max Expectation = E(f(p)) by gradiant descent |
